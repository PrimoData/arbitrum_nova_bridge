Background
Arbitum is a layer 2 Ethereum scaling techonology built by Offchain Labs. Arbitrum One is its first blockchain and one of the more popular ETH L2s. Arbitrum recently launched its second blockchain, Nova, and made it avaliable to the public on August 9th, 2022. The purpose of Nova is to have faster and cheaper transactions than Ethereum and Arbitrum One, with a focus on gaming and social. Users can bridged tokens from Ethereum using the Arbitrum bridge.
Purpose
The purpose of this dashboard and analysis is to understand user adoption/growth of Nova by looking at Ethereum -> Arbitrum Nova bridging activity.
